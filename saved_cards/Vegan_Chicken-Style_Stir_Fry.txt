Vegan Chicken-Style Stir Fry
Ingredients:
 - vegan chicken substitute
 - celery
 - soy sauce
 - olive oil
 - garlic
 - ginger

Allergens:
 - soy
 - gluten

Steps:
 - Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
 - Add minced garlic and ginger, saut√© for 1 minute.
 - Add sliced celery and cook for 3-4 minutes until tender.
 - Stir in vegan chicken substitute and soy sauce, cook for an additional 5 minutes.
 - Serve hot over rice or noodles.

Time: 20 minutes
